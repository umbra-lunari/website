Umbra Lunari

A brazilian New/Alternative Metal band.

About


With heavy inspiration in Deftones, the bassist, Andrey Tavares (A.K.A. Monochrome) founded the band on february/2023.

At first time, the band was called "Nightfall", but, after huge conversations, they changed the name to Umbra Lunari.

The Members

Singer

Lead singer of the band, with strong tremble and sweet voice at the verses. Maybe she is the woman version of Chino Moreno

Gabi Bayo

Lead Guitarrist

Inspirated by Stephen Carpenter, he is the lead guitarrist at all. He usually do most of guitar fills, but, with his 7-string guitar, he brings amazing and powerful riffs.

Brenno Barbosa

Rhythm Guitarrist

Inspirated by Tim Henson from Polyphia, he is the rhythm guitarrist at all. Most of songs he plays the rhythm, bringing the base of the song, but, with his 6-string guitar, he do powerful and organic solos with amazing tone.

Héricles Paiva

Bassist

Bassist who brings powerful grooves to the song. Futhermore, he plays the synth, do all the screamo backvocals and are the band's music producer.

Andrey Tavares

Drummer

Strong drummer who seek play with perfection most of drums' lines. His grip and posture on the drums are capable of breaking the venue. A truly goat.
